About Me

I’m a self-motivated full-stack software engineer with a passion for crafting engaging products that make an impact. After graduating from General Assembly’s Software Engineering Immersive and Codecademy’s Full Stack Developer Immersive, I’ve continued to blend technology, artistry, and leadership across every project.

My background as a creative director and project manager means I thrive in collaborative environments. I love transforming complex ideas into clear solutions—whether that’s designing a digital product, building out a software feature, or leading a team through a production schedule.

Beyond software, I co-direct Paramount Winterguard in Atlanta and serve as Artistic Director for The Cavaliers Drum and Bugle Corps. These roles keep my creative perspective sharp and reinforce the importance of storytelling, mentorship, and community.
