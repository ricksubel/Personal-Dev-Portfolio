About Me

  • I’m a passionate and self-motivated Full Stack Software Engineer with a strong foundation in building interactive, user-focused web applications. A graduate of General Assembly’s Software Engineering Immersive and Codeacademy’s Full Stack Developer Immersive, I bring a creative mindset and a drive for continuous learning to every project I take on. My background as a creative director and project manager gives me a unique ability to combine technical expertise with strong leadership, problem-solving, and design skills. I thrive in collaborative environments, excel at turning complex ideas into workable solutions, and am dedicated to creating innovative, engaging, and reliable digital experiences.

    In addition to my coding and software interest, I am the Co-Founder, Co-Director, and Creative Designer for Paramount Winterguard in Atlanta, GA. I am also the Artistic Director for The Cavaliers Drum and Bugle Corps, from Rosemont, IL. As an active pageantry designer, I am constantly learning and sharing new and proven processes for creating competitive and artistic designs. In addition to my pageantry design roles, I am an active adjudicator, clinician, and consultant.
